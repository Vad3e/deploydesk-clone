Service Request Tracking
Submit, track, and manage event requests with full details — venue, members, capacity, and scheduling all in one place.

Capacity-Constrained Allocation
Our CCAA engine automatically validates every request against member workload, venue availability, and concurrent event limits.

Schedule Monitoring
View weekly and monthly availability for all members. Color-coded conflict indicators keep your team on track.

Monthly, weekly, and daily calendar views with drag-and-drop rescheduling for admins and clickable event details.

Email Notifications
Automated email triggers for submissions, approvals, rejections, reminders, and schedule changes — keeping everyone informed.

Role-Based Access Control
Separate dashboards and permissions for Admins and Members. Secure JWT authentication with input validation and CSRF protection.
